## **1\. Information We Collect**

We may collect and process the following information:

## **2\. Use of Cookies**

- **What are cookies?** Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a site to improve your browsing experience.
- **How we use cookies:** We use cookies to help us understand user behavior, which helps us improve our website. Cookies may also enhance your user experience by, for example, remembering your preferences and settings.
- **Third-party cookies:** We might have third-party services on our site (like analytics) that use cookies to collect data about your usage. This helps us understand our audience better and provide relevant content.
- **Managing cookies:** Most browsers allow you to manage cookies through their settings. Remember, if you disable cookies, some features of our site may not work as intended.

## **3\. How We Use the Information**

The information we collect may be used in the following ways:
